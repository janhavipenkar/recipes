Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup veg oil
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 1 tsp vanilla
 - 2 large eggs
 - 1/4 tsp beaking powder
 - 1/3 cup cocoa powder
 - 1/4 tsp salt
 - 1/2 cup flour

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour 9 x 9 inch square baking pan.
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, mix oil and sugar until blended.Add eggs and vanilla, stir just until blended.
 
 3. Mix all dry ingredients in a seperate bowl and combine it with oil and sugar mixture.

 4.Pour into baking pan and bake for 20 min. or until sides just start to pull away from the pan. Cool compleletely before cutting.
